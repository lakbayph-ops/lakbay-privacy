Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Lakbay (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Lakbay mobile application (“App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address (via third-party sign-in)
  • Profile information from authentication providers
  • Booking and itinerary-related information

We do not store passwords. Authentication is handled by third-party services.

2.2 Location Information

With your permission, the App may collect approximate or precise location data to:

  • Suggest nearby destinations and accommodations
  • Generate travel routes and itineraries
  • Improve navigation accuracy

Location access is optional and can be disabled through your device settings at any time.

2.3 Usage & Technical Data

We may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system, app usage statistics, and crash logs to improve performance and stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve core app features
  • Process bookings and travel itineraries
  • Enhance search and route recommendations
  • Maintain app security and reliability

4. Payments

Payments are processed entirely by third-party payment providers. Lakbay does not store or process credit card numbers, bank details, or payment credentials.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Limited information may be shared only with:

  • Authentication providers
  • Map and navigation services
  • Payment processors
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for location access
  • Stop using the App at any time

9. Children’s Privacy

Lakbay is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
